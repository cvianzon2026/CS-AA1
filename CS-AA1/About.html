Coziness Cafe was founded by its founder, Carlitos Mateo G. Vianzon, when he was thinking of a cafe for tired PSHS-CLC Students. He opened its first establishment in Lily Hill, Clark Freeport, Pampanga in 2021. Now there are (as of November 27, 2022) 58 locations all around the Philippines, varying from Session Road in Baguio to Davao City!
